Cold Brew Concentrate Recipe

December 29, 2025

This recipe produces coffee concentrate that should be diluted 1 part concentrate, 2 parts water/milk. It keeps well in the fridge for up to a week.

Ingredients

  • 100 g coffee beans
  • 800 ml water

Method

  1. Coarsely grind the coffee beans. Put the ground coffee into a glass jar, at least 1.5 litre capacity.
  2. Add 800 ml of room temperature water.
  3. Let stand at room temperature for 12 hours. Note: anywhere from 8-16 hours is okay. Room temperature tested for 10°C to 25°C.
  4. Use paper filters to decant the concentrate from the coffee residue and oil.
