Biscoff Hojicha Miso Cookies

December 15, 2025

These cookies combine the caramelized sweetness of Biscoff spread with the earthy notes of hojicha and the savory depth of white miso. The result is a complex, sophisticated cookie that balances sweet, nutty, and umami flavors.

Cookie Dough

Ingredients

  • 80 g softened unsalted butter
  • 45 g Biscoff spread
  • 120 g granulated sugar
  • 90 g light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg (room temp)
  • 4.5 g hojicha powder
  • 12 g white (shiro) miso
  • 180 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Method

  1. Whisk flour, baking soda, salt, hojicha powder. Set aside.
  2. Cream butter, Biscoff spread, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy (2â€“3 min)
  3. Add egg, mix just until emulsified
  4. Add miso, beat until completely dissolved and smooth
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix just until combined
  6. Wrap in cling wrap, chill in fridge for 12-24 hours

Baking

  1. Set oven to 170C, conventional.
  2. Scoop slightly larger than ice cream scoop.
  3. Bake 11 minutes. Pull when edges are set, centre soft, tops matte, not glossy
  4. Cool on tray 5 min, then rack.

Note: When baking from frozen, add 1-2 minutes. Do not thaw first.
