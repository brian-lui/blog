Songs with great basslines

February 24, 2025

Here are some famous basslines from songs that should be fairly well known but are still great:

Feel Good Inc - Gorillaz - it's a good bassline sir

Billie Jean - Michael Jackson - it's a good bassline sir

Hysteria - Muse - this is harder to play than it sounds

Is This It - The Strokes - I like the stuff starting around 0:54

Get Lucky - Daft Punk - this bassline is mostly good because it sounds good

Here are some less famous songs that I like. Not for everyone. But I have very good taste in basslines, so they are pretty good.

Lauren - Men I Trust - it's a good bassline sir

Show Me How - Men I Trust - good bassline and also good chords. This combination is creating shareholder value

Giant Steps - John Coltrane - the bass is like a very competent guard dog here

Maps - Emancipator - triphop/downtempo songs generally have good basslines, because they can't go hog wild with the percussion or the guitars

Laser - Roman Tam - dance song. With lasers

Il Vento d'Oro - Yugo Kanno - the whole song is kind of wild but in a friendly way

Thanks for reading my blog!
